Anywhere and Everywhere

The project aim was to create a series typographic visuals that would be inserted into a newspaper about the chosen theme, which was travel anxiety.

I focused on breaking the ‘rules’ of typography to portray the mental journey of someone who experiences travel anxiety at the station. You see the emotional journey start off quite stressful, then becomes more relaxed. The overlapping and busy type is contrasted with bright, relaxing gradients to create a campaign which ultimately spreads a reassuring message that you are not alone and it is okay to feel this way. The message also reminds viewers that travelling can end up feeling like an escape for some people too.
