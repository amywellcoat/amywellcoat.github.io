about me


email

Hello! I'm Amy Wellcoat

I have recently achieved a BA in Visual Communication and Digital Publishing with Distinction. My skills specialise in graphic design and illustration.

My style of work is colourful and contemporary which has formed over years of development whilst working on a variety of project types. I utilise my skills in traditional and digital creation and enjoy uniquely combining them when I can. My creative process is well practiced by the range of briefs I have worked on which you can see displayed in my portfolio. Using my sharp skills throughout the Adobe Creative Cloud, I approach each brief with a range of ideas and attention to detail whilst striving to design creative and stylish solutions.

Having worked as part of a design agency with classmates, I developed useful experience in organisation and teamwork skills and I am keen to learn more about working in the industry. Please don't hesitate to get in touch if you have any questions or would like to work with me!

CV available on request.
