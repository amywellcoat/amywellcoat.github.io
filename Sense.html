Sense Digital Magazine

The project brief was to design a publication which features interactive elements such as sounds, buttons and links.

After having done a bit of research into mental health, I decided to create a digital magazine which would raise awareness and provide a self help outlet. I published a range of Q&A's and polls on my social media to gather anonymous information about the way people deal with their emotions when feeling a bit down. Most answers spoke about going outside, doing outdoor activities and how hearing and seeing certain things positively affected their feelings. Therefore, I decided that the content should inform the reader about how they can utilise their senses to deal with anxieties.

In order to create an enjoyable and informative magazine for mental health, it also needed to have relaxing visuals and sounds throughout. The visuals were created using relaxing muted colours, illustrations and photographic images relating to the topics, all combined together to create contemporary collages. These minimalistic visuals are accompanied by relaxing sounds, recorded first hand whilst outside walking and listening to my surroundings. Based on my research; the sounds of nature, birds, ocean waves, wind and rain are the most satisfying.

Towards the end of the publication there is an informative help page for anyone who needs it, which includes a collection of links to other websites and charities who can help. By using iBooks Author, I was able to easily add these buttons and hyper-links to provide a fully interactive user experience whilst reading and learning from the magazine.


Download Sense on Apple Books (iOS only): https://books.apple.com/gb/book/sense/id1481101468
