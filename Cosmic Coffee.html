Cosmic Coffee

This was a collaboration with a fellow designer where we briefed ourselves for a personal project together. The task was to create our own modern coffee brand and packaging for three different flavours, using various materials which could ultimately be reused.

We brainstormed ideas and each took on our own roles. Together we designed simple branding which would suit the contemporary visuals. We decided that we wanted the graphics to have a fun, illustrative and colourful style. I fully illustrated the packaging for the three designs based on the origin of the coffees, whilst my colleague arranged the compositions with text for design mock-ups. The core packaging is designed to be made from tin, and the design will be on a sleeve that can slide off after the coffee tin is no longer needed. Therefore, the sleeve will have a second use and can become a translucent, stylish candle holder.
