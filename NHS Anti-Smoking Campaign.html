NHS Anti-Smoking Campaign

This was a live brief set to our degree design agency by Fife College and NHS Fife. The task was to create a campaign that would inform people of the advantages of not starting to smoke.

After coming up with a range of initial ideas, the client picked our concept of an interactive PDF displayed on an iPad with a supporting social media campaign. Another colleague and myself illustrated the character and his organs which can each be clicked on to display the benefits of not smoking within that area. The final interactive app was created using Adobe XD. We also ran a social media campaign called "Don't Even Start" across Instagram and Facebook where we quizzed the followers every day with factual polls and questions about the benefits of not smoking. The information was provided directly by NHS Fife. This allowed us to collect first hand research for them; what people's opinions were on the matter, and highlighting what topics or demographics they should target in the future. The final solutions were displayed and demoed at the recent "No Smoking Event" at Fife College and will continue to be used by NHS Fife in their Don't Start Smoking campaign.
