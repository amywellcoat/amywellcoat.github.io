Fantasy Cities

Using the prompt "Fantasy City", the aim was to create a triptych of mixed media pieces by combining urban and rural landscapes.

During first hand research of places such as Newcastle and Glencoe, the idea was to combine the contrasting areas and create a fresh, dream-like scene. Through experimentation with different techniques such as lino, embroidery, embellishing, drawing and painting, each element was collated together and framed with large negative space. The fore, middle and backgrounds of each composition are accentuated by the use of 3D foam to lift up areas such as the mountains and the outer frames. This adds a great deal of depth to the overall artwork and forms a realistic 3D perspective of the scenery. The circular windows frame the striking artwork, creating 'portals' that look directly into these colourful fantasy landscapes.
