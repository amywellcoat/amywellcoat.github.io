eResonance Stress Reduction Cards

This is an amazing collaboration with eResonance who are a brand that provide quality products which help women to combat obstacles such as stress, anxiety and panic. Working together with over 40 other artists from around the world, we have each created a card for this limited edition Stress Reduction Essentials Deck. Each card has it's own artwork for an individual topic, with tips and tricks on the reverse side.All profits from this project go towards the amazing charity CAMFED who are revolutionising the way education is delivered to girls in Africa.

The theme I was given was "imagining a happy place". On my social media platforms, I collected anonymous answers on what/where my followers felt their happy place is. Most of the answers included being near water and hills, being with friends and watching a beautiful sunset, so I decided to combine each of these elements into a digital illustration with a magical ambience for my card design.


Purchase the full set of cards from eResonance here: https://eresonance.myshopify.com/collections/the-stress-reduction-set-1/products/eart2020-stress-reduction-deck
